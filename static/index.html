The Problem with Discord Applications

Screenshots can be faked. Word of mouth can be unreliable. When someone applies for roles in a Discord server, how do you prove they actually did the work?

Unverifiable Claims

Anyone can claim they were an admin, a contributor, or a designer. Without proof, your application is just words.

Lost History

Servers get deleted. You leave teams. Your hard work disappears into the void.

Verified History

Request endorsements from server owners or admins to verify your roles. Build credibility with confirmed data.

Instant Portfolio

Generate a beautiful, shareable link that showcases your entire Discord career. Perfect for apply for roles in any Discord servers.

Secure & Private

You fully control what to display. Your data is secure and only shared with your permission. Trusted by communities worldwide.
