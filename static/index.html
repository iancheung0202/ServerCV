Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is ServerCV free to use?

Yes, it's free to start! For power users, Premium unlocks unlimited experiences, more customization options, vanity URLs for your user and server profiles, and more.

How do endorsements work?

You can request an endorsement from any server where you are currently a Server Owner, Administrator, or Moderator. Once submitted, a Server Owner or Administrator of that server can review and approve your request.

Who can approve requests?

Only Server Owners and Administrators can view and manage requests. Server Owners can approve any request (including their own). Administrators can approve requests from Moderators, but cannot approve requests from other Administrators or the Owner.

Can I control my portfolio?

Yes. You can delete and update the "End Date" of any experience from your profile at any time. Premium users can also pin their most important experiences to the top.

To ensure credibility, you cannot edit any other details of an approved experience yourself. If your role changes, a Server Owner or Administrator can update your role title for you. If your experience is edited with incorrect information, you can simply delete that experience and submit a new endorsement request.

What about privacy?

Your portfolio is public and can be shared with anyone. We only display the information you explicitly request and get approved. You can delete an entry at any time to remove it from your public profile.
